Our Services

    Lessons

    Private and Small Group Volleyball Training

    Katie offers private coaching lessons for volleyball players. Lessons are for players who are looking for additional reps and individual, specialized coaching. Specific skill technique instruction is provided to help players advance their game to the next level. Players are also exposed to development in leadership, confidence-building, mental toughness and teamwork. And above all else, lessons are meant to be fun, supportive and show players they are worthy humans.

    Consulting Services

    All services are designed to offer guidance dependent on each athlete's age and desired amount of support.
